
 　　【摘要】 目的 探讨护理干预在激光美容术后患者防晒中的应用效果。方法 80例行激光美容术患者， 按照不同的护理方式分为对照组和观察组， 各40例。对照组患者给予常规护理， 观察组患者给予全面的防晒护理干预。比较两组患者护理后汉密尔顿抑郁量表（HAMD）、汉密尔顿焦虑量表（HAMA）、生活质量（QOL）评分以及并发症发生情况。结果 护理前两组患者HAMD、HAMA以及QOL量表评分比较差异无统计学（P>0.05）；护理后， 观察组患者HAMD、HAMA评分低于对照组， QOL量表评分高于对照组， 差异具有统计学意义（P<0.05）。护理6个月后随访， 观察组患者并发症发生率为5.0%， 低于对照组的20.0%， 差异具有统计学意义（χ2=4.114， P<0.05）。结论 激光美容术后实施防晒护理干预能够有效减轻患者焦虑和抑郁等不良心理状况， 提高患者的生活质量， 降低并发症发生率。

　　【Abstract】 Objective To investigate application effect by nursing intervention in sun block for patients after cosmetic laser surgery. Methods A total of 80 patients receiving cosmetic laser surgery were divided by different nursing measures into control group and observation group， with 40 cases in each group. The control group received conventional nursing， and the observation group received comprehensive sun block nursing intervention. Comparison was made on scores of Hamilton depression scale （HAMD）， Hamilton anxiety scale （HAMA）， quality of life （QOL） score， and complications between the two groups after nursing. Results There was no statistically significant difference of HAMD， HAMA and QOL scores between the two groups before nursing （P>0.05）. After nursing， the observation group had lower HAMD and HAMA scores， and higher QOL score than the control group， and their difference had statistical significance （P<0.05）. Follow-up after 6 months of nursing showed lower incidence of complications in the observation group as 5.0% than 20.0% in the control group， and the difference had statistical significance （χ2=4.114， P<0.05）. Conclusion Implement of sun block nursing intervention after cosmetic laser surgery can effectively relieve adverse psychological status of depression and anxiety， improve quality of life in patients and reduce ineidence of complications.
　　【Key words】 Cosmetic laser surgery； Nursing intervention； Sun block nursing； Application effect
